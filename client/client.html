Blob Attack

This game takes inspiration from the game: Achtung Die Kurve. The game is a kind of multi-player snake where a player wins when he/she is the last player alive. One does not grow by eating food as in the traditional snake game but grow by merely staying alive. There are no weapons or other things to kill the other players, instead each player kill the other players by trapping them so they either move out of bounds or into a snake path. In other words: Collision means death.
